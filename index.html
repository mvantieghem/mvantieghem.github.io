Michelle VanTieghem

About

I have a PhD in Psychology with expertise in quantitative data analysis and bio-behavioral health research. I am passionate about applying data science tools for reproducible research, and I have taught several workshops and courses on data cleaning, analysis, and visualization using R programming.

Outside of work, I'm an avid cyclist and also blog about concussion awareness and prevention.
